    Hi! I'm Aaron Stern,a computer science student and D3 swimmer in my final year at Dickinson College. I have a strong foundation in frontend and back-end development and thrive in dynamic environments where I can leverage my technical skills and creativity to bring ideas to life.


    During my academic and professional journey, I've gained hands-on experience in various areas of software development. In my previous role as a Full-Stack Developer Intern at The Brooker Group, I played a pivotal role in developing and maintaining the frontend of Brooker's new generative AI platform, using technologies such as Vue.js, Vuetify, and AWS Amplify.


    Before that, as a Frontend Software Engineer Intern at Vendii, TAO BIN, I worked on a project to improve the cleint dashboard and create forms for clients to easily manage the state of their vending machines. I utilized tools like React, TypeScript, and Material-UI.


    When I'm not coding, you can find me swimming laps in the pool, perfecting my swing on the golf course with friends, or cheering on my boy Alex Albon in Formula One. I also enjoy staying up-to-date with the latest tech trends and innovations and constantly seek opportunities to learn and grow personally and professionally. I'm excited about the endless possibilities that the world of technology offers and am passionate about exploring new avenues in this ever-evolving field.
